This section documents the creation and verification of Distribution Lists, which are used to broadcast emails to a group of people without requiring a shared inbox.

Overview: Distribution List

- Does not store emails (it simply forwards them to members).

- Does not have a calendar or a physical folder.

- Is ideal for one-way announcements or simple team notifications.

## 1. Creating a distribiuton list

Distribution lists are configured via the Microsoft 365 Admin Center under Teams & groups > Active teams & groups.

- Step A: Select the Distribution list tab and initiate the creation wizard.

- Step B: Define the list name, primary email address, and description.

- Step C: Assign Owners (who can manage the list) and Members (who will receive the emails).
